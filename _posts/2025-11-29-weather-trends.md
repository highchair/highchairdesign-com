Last November, I wrote that it was unseasonably warm. Well, this year it is the opposite. Maybe not unseasonably cold — more like what is probably normal for this time of the season.

August felt like a bummer. Summer was warm but cooled off quickly. August had cooler days, fewer sun breaks, and more rain.

September was pretty good but cool — again, probably more like the summer’s of a decade ago. October was nice as well, with Halloween being the right amount of chilly but not too chilly to walk around in a costume for an hour.
